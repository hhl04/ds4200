My Favorite Movie Franchise

ALIEN

With the latest installment in the franchise, Alien: Romulus, being a box office hit gathering $350 million worldwide, this cosmos-spanning franchise has received more attention from old and new fans alike. I dedicate this website to briefly list and explain all the main events of the Alien mytho, spanning five centuries across different movies, novels and video games.

My first exposure to this franchise was in 2017, when I watched Prometheus and Alien: Covenant, both were not the best works to exist in this franchise.
Compared to previous entries, these two movies were experimental and trying to add more myths to the popular-but-familiar Xenomorph mythos. The newly added myths were controversial but logical, but what drew the most criticism was the poorly written and executed scripts of these movies. I love the newly added myths, but am not fond of the poor screenplay.
